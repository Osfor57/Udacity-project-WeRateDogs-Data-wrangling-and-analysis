The data was collected and cleaned in order to analyize it , this process was oriented by two main questions:¶

1. How was the WeRateDogs trending (deppending on like and retweet count of each tweet)?¶

2. What is the qualities(dog breed, dog stage and rating values) of the most trending rating tweet?¶

  • The key metrics of these analyses was :
    1. Retweets count
    2. Likes count
  • Qualities provided by each rating tweet:
    1. Dog breed
    2. Dog stage
    3. Rating of the dog

Starting by the first question: 1. How was the WeRateDogs trending (deppending on like and retweet count of each tweet)?¶

By making a double line graph (found in wrangle_act.ipynb file) between the timestamps from data collected in the x-axis
and retweets counts and likes counts in the y-axis I found that:

  • In the interval between 2016-05 and 2016-07, WeRateDogs page was most trending.
  • Also I noticed that there are generaly four intervals that the page was Trending at (have peaks in retweet and like counts,
    those four intervals are:
    1. 2015-11 to 2016-01
    2. 2016-05 to 2016-07 (the most trendig period)
    3. 2016-11 to 2017-03
    4. 2017-05 to 2017-09

Attempting to answer the second question: 2. What is the qualities(dog breed, dog stage and rating values) of the most trending rating tweet?¶

In order to answer this question, I tried measuring every quality with the two key metrics¶
